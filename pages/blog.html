Impact Of Technology On Daily Life

Imagine a world with no phones, computers or any other gadgets?


The first question that comes up while imagining is, is it even possible? Well life on earth would still be possible but what wouldn't be possible is the way we are staying right now.


Technology has revolutionized our entire human society. Whether it is from buying groceries online to using it for various innovations and major discoveries. Technologies has made one's life much easier and fulfilling. Technological innovations have been playing a major role since a long decade and has benefited the human society in a major way. People nowadays are not just using technology to connect with one another. Indeed, it is more of connected to one's own personal daily life and also various other aspects of business.


Our daily life is entirely depended on various technologies right now. It has changed the way we behave, operate and interact in our daily life. With the use of it people are able to reach a much more comfortable position. This includes our daily use of “computers on wheels” (modern cars) for traveling from one place to another and use of different gadgets for numerous purposes (communication, entertainment, information, etc.). If we think deeply, everything we use and surround ourselves by in our day-to-day life is a product of technology. Various advancements in different fields such as business, medical, engineering is with the help through technological innovations.

Impact Of Technology On Ecommerce Website


Let's dive deeper into the relation of technology in the field of business. Business is not just buying and selling of product and services at a marketplace in a certain time. There is more complexity than this. All of those which have been briefed and summed up into an easier way through the help of technology. We can all agree that technology has made communicating business and various other business processes more reliable and easier. Well, adding on its technology has given each and every person no matter which part of the world be involved in the field of business. It has helped encouraging people to have their own business and operate it with much comfort. Now with certain skillsets and use of technology everyone is able to claim themselves as an entrepreneur or a business(man/woman).


Technology has also helped well established brands operate with much more effectiveness and efficiency. It has helped the businesses in gaining much more customer base and engagement, creating simple and effective team structure and processes, directing and leading their employees, providing various services to both employees and customers etc. Nowadays, a major part of business success includes how well it has been able to integrate and utilize the value package and benefits brought by our technological advancements.


Talking about the impacts of technology, it nearly influences every aspect of our human lives. The impact of technology is so severe that without its existence we would simply be living the era of ancient period. Without any technological innovations our society would regress at least 50 years in the past. Technology has simply brought convenience in one's life. But we all know to everything lies two sides; a positive and negative.

Positive Impact


The positive side that has been brought by the advancements in technology lies faster and much easier way to communicate with people in different part of the world. Technology streamlines daily tasks from grocery shopping to online banking making one's life more convenient. Technology has also served as a medium to access different existing information out there and also playing a major role in finding new discoveries in a much more reliable and accurate way as possible. Other than that technology also enables us to different entertainment medias and likely in health and fitness.


For a business world, technology enables businesses to reach a global audience expanding their market reach. Along with that it also fuels innovation allowing firms to develop new products and services to reach in a competitive position. In today's world technology has also majorly contributed in data analytics so as to understand one's customer behavior and have information regarding their past, present and potential customer base. Similarly, digital communication tools and various platforms facilitate in remote works and enable all team members to collaborate in an effective manner.

Negative Impact


It seems like technology has been one of the greatest innovations till now. Well even though it is true to some extent but behind every advancement lies a consideration for potential drawbacks. First and foremost, the major drawback that people are facing is simply due to social isolation. With the excessive use of technological innovations people are being more and more less sociable and relying on technology for everything. This has also led to some people being lazy and fully dependent on it for everything. A negative aspect of technology for people using it not appropriately is that people are addicted to it and waste most part of their time being useless. This can be also termed as digital addiction.


In a business world to though the impact of technology lies major in the good side however, it has also raised issues such as cybersecurity threats. This includes hacking, malware, fishing which not only severely harms a business but also impacts all of the stakeholders' raising concerns regarding data privacy. Another rising problem that can be seen in today's world is with the advancement in artificial intelligent and various other technological innovations, people can be prone to job displacement and unemployment. The world would simply be running by machines in the future time if this remains in continuity.

Conclusion

As we can see, technology has both pros and cons to it but what differs its output is simply how a person is using those advancements. With a proper utilization of these technological tools people can enlighten their daily life into a much safer and a convenient stay. In the field of business technology can be used in development of various firms and it really opens the doors of various opportunities if used effectively. However, with a careless use and an inappropriate use of technology, we would have to face severe consequences which is far beyond our imagination. Technology withholds the power to both develop and destroy our lives in a short period of time.
